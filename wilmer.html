SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS

The scholarship application period is now open. The next scholarship application cycle will last until September 2023 for the courses starting in 2024.

Wilmer Academy-administered scholarships are available for Wilmer campuses nationwide.

  • Applicants are considered for all available scholarships
  • Wilmer Academy previous scholarship award recipients are only eligible for one scholarship award per year
  • All applications are submitted through the Wilmer Academy “online” scholarship portal
  • Applications are accepted ONLY during the application period

The number of scholarships awarded, as well as the benefits of each award, will vary by the number of qualified applicants received.

ELIGIBILITY

AFRICAN CITIZENS and WEST AFRICA PERMANENT RESIDENTS

Wilmer Academy-administered scholarships are available for Wilmer campuses nationwide.

  • On-Campus programs at the Calabar or Cape Town Campuses only
  • Gemology Lab Classes at the Lagos or Dakar Campuses only
  • Online Courses
  • Eligible for a wide Age range

HOW TO APPLY - ONLINE ONLY

  • Wilmer Academy scholarship application form is available only during the open application period.
  • Required documents if any must be in Word, pdf or jpeg format (rtf, odt, txt, msg documents, zip folders are NOT acceptable).
  • Complete only one online scholarship application form, answer all questions and attach required documents if any.
  • Applicant must indicate below a program of study and the method of study (on-campus, lab class, or online courses).
  • All applicants not chosen for a scholarship, or those who decline a scholarship, are encouraged to apply again.
